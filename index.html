Our Story
Born from a Love
of Real Flavour

Churn Station was founded with one simple belief — that Lahore deserved world-class ice cream made right here at home. We source the finest ingredients, churn every batch with care, and serve it with pride across three branches.

From our signature Belgian Chocolate to the fan-favourite Lotus Cheesecake, each flavour is crafted to be an experience. Stop by Gulshan-e-Ravi, Sabzazar or Iqbal Town — or just order in.
